ABOUT GEORGE BROWN COLLEGE:

With currently three campuses located throughout Toronto, George Brown College is an esteemed institution committed to student development.
The college provides many forms of support through various sectors such as food, finances, academics and more.
A particular centre entitled "Career Services" proves to be very beneficial as it aids students with job preparation.
Additonally, the Student Association provides opportunities for students to volunteer, form connections and more.

Without doubt, George Brown College provides many offers to students and will continue to have a great community.
